About us

The founder Indira Basapa started First Steps (a Pre-primary school) No. 12, Cunningham Crescent Road, Bangalore – 560052 in the year 1984-85. A branch (Pre-primary) was started in the year 2007 at Jakkur – North Bangalore. Over the years we grew to accommodate a grades school – Jain Public School, Jakkur in the same campus in 2014.    Read More →
