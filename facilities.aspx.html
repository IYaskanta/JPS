Facilities

We offer an environment Jainites need in order to learn and grow.

JPS offers everything they need to support their learning and growth. Smart classrooms, well-equipped science and computer laboratories, a vast library, auditorium, and generous play areas meet their every educational need.

Infrastructure : Creating learning friendly spaces

Our building features spacious, well- lit and ventilated classrooms. Supported learning sessions take place in spacious learning labs. Computer labs and well equipped science laboratories give students hands-on experience and the technological expertise required for a digital world. Auditorium provides the ideal setting for school programs and workshops. Our well stocked library is designed to encourage reading and research.

Transport

Ensuring safe and convenient travel for students. JPS owns and operates a fleet of buses equipped with all safety measures mandated by law, GPS, fire extinguishers, CCTV cameras, a first – aid box and speed governors. Our transport has devised structured emergency policies and procedures and all our drivers and attendants are police – verified. Bus routes cover many key areas of South Bangalore.

Class Rooms

JPS facilitates spacious, modern classrooms. Each classroom houses a large area and is designed such that every student gets the teacher’s individual attention.

There are over 25 classrooms for regular teaching and a number of other rooms like the Audio Visual rooms and Resource rooms to facilitate better learning. Ergonomically designed colourful furniture adds to the ambience of the classroom.

Learning Resource center

Library

The library is the main resource center for the students and the faculty at Jain Public school. The library has a repository of over 15,000 volumes comprising of textbooks, reference books, fiction titles, periodicals and audio – visual materials like CDs and VCDs covering a gamut of subjects and topics; all aimed at making the experience of learning enjoyable.

A special reading programme for the students of Grades 1 to 6 encourages children to read recommended age appropriate books.

Computer Lab

Computer education is an integral part of the curriculum at Jain Public School. The state-of the art computer laboratory is one of its kind. Students are trained to learn the latest software applications and programming languages.

The school offers computer education from Class 1 to Class 8. Highly qualified teachers impart the knowledge of computers and equip the students for the challenges of the computer age. A dedicated broadband Internet connection in the lab provides Internet access to websites for educational purposes.

The Science Laboratory

An ultramodern science Lab at Jain Public School provides an atmosphere of experiential learning. The Science labs also provide an environment for students to develop a scientific temperament through experiments and research work.

Under the guidance and assistance given by our science teachers, students are encouraged to carry out research work to exhibit their innovations by developing models.

The Math Lab

A well-equipped Math Lab at JPS provides students with the opportunity to have fun with numbers. It creates a challenging atmosphere for students to instill and develop observation skills among them, to put their mathematical competencies to test and enhance their analytical reasoning and lateral thinking through various games and activities.

The Art & Craft Programme

Art plays a vital role in the all-round development of the students towards moulding the thought process and values of life. The spacious art room at JPS helps to unleash the creative streak in students. The soothing ambience makes it a much sought after room.

Audio-Visual Rooms

Computer-Aided teaching sessions hold a special place at JPS which gives our students the winning edge in a competitive world.

The audio-visual room is extensively used by the faculty and the students to make presentations. To ensure long-term retention of what is taught, school uses Power Point presentations and Smart Board to hone student skills and encourage student-teacher interactions.

Auditorium

Auditorium is meant for all public and school functions. The large stage with a green room is frequently used for plays as well as other stage presentations. The LCD projector, state-of-the-art sound system and lights enable students to present various activities and programmes.

Infirmary

JPS has an infirmary which is looked after by qualified nurse staff. Medical check-ups of students are regularly carried out and record of the same is maintained. If the doctor is of the opinion that a ward needs special medical attention, the parents are immediately informed.

Guidance and Counselling

Qualified and experienced teachers act as counsellors and provide assistance to the children with respect to learning. They guide the students by positive intervention to address emotional and behavioural issues observed in them at school.


Safety at school

The safety of our children is paramount. Designing a school security system is a delicate balance of maintaining a free, open, welcoming, creative, and educational atmosphere while still offering a secure location for kids to learn and ease parents’ worries.

  • Access Control at Entrances and Exits. When students are arriving or leaving, all points of movements are monitored by teachers, staff, or administrators as well as cameras. During class time, all outer doors are locked, and all visitors have a single point of access, preferably directly into the main office so they can check in. An intercom is installed with a camera at the primary entrance to verify a visitor’s identity and purpose for visiting before unlocking this entrance and allowing anyone into the building. The school issues photo ID cards to all students, as well as Parent ID and Escort ID cards for parents and others authorised to pick students up after school.
  • Supply closets, electrical cabinets, A/C and heating unit areas, and other critical locations within a school are also access controlled.
  • We have an efficient network of the CCTV system throughout the school to monitor for external and internal threats to student safety. Incidents involving hostile visitors, inappropriate behaviour, bullying, fighting, and other hazards that can harm students can be caught on camera and properly addressed during and after such an event. Plus, any entry points can be observed at all times using this technology.
  • The school has a Child Protection Policy in line with the POCSO (Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act). As mandated by the Education Department and the Police, the school has instituted a Child Protection Committee consisting of a Child Protection Officer, the school Principal, one child safety expert and two staff members. The CPO has been appointed as the first point of contact in case of any untoward incident occurring in the campus
